HYPS Band

Info

James Alyn Wee and Karn Kasidej Hongladaromp didn’t think their friendship could be interrupted for as long as eight years. They also didn’t expect that the period of separation caused by the Covid pandemic would inadvertently bring them back in touch, allowing them to find comfort and harmony in music. The result of this reunion was the formation of the band HYBS, as well as their debut album "Making Steak." What surprised them even more was that their work resonated not only in Thailand but also on a global scale.
